Using Room Funds

There is $75 per person available for room parties until funds run out.
  • Create a proposal, consisting of an itemized budget including date, time, and location of the room party, and send to bakerexec@mit.edu
  • 75% of the participants in the room party must come to the exec meeting at least 4 days before
  • All remaining food from the party must be sent to the front desk, and remaining supplies and decorations go to Baker
  • Only Baker residents may be invited, and all Baker residents must be invited (send an email to baker-forum or baker-general 5-7 days before)
